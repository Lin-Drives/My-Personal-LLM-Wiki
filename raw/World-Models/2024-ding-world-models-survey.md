# Understanding World or Predicting Future? A Comprehensive Survey of World Models

> Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.14499
> Collected: 2026-05-31
> Published: 2024-11-21 (v1), revised 2025-12-10 (v4). ACM Computing Surveys.

**Authors:** Jingtao Ding, Yunke Zhang, Yu Shang, Jie Feng, et al. (15 authors)

## Abstract

The paper surveys world models, noting their role in constructing internal representations to understand the mechanisms of the world and predicting future states to simulate and guide decision-making. It examines applications in generative games, autonomous driving, robotics, and social simulacra, and summarizes key challenges and provides insights into potential future research directions. The survey organizes the literature along two functional axes: Understanding (building internal representations to grasp world mechanisms) and Prediction (forecasting future states to guide decisions). It covers the complete evolution from Ha & Schmidhuber (2018) "World Models" through LeCun's JEPA vision to Sora and diffusion-based world models.
